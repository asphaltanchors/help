title: Return Policy
description: Asphalt Anchors return policy
---

Returns are accepted within 60 days of shipment for non-defective items, subject to:

* 20% restocking fee
* Original prepaid freight charges (if applicable)

## Return Requirements

* Items must be unused and in original packaging
* Customer pays return shipping
* Include copy of sales receipt
* Email tracking number to orders@asphaltanchors.com

## Return Address 

MDC Warehousing and Distribution
Attn: Asphalt Anchor Returns
225 Long Ave, Building 15
Hillside, NJ 07205

Refunds are processed to the original payment method after inspection at our warehouse.
