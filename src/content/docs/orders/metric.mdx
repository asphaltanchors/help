Our online store is focused on US only and imperial sizes. Metric sizes are available through our non‑US distributors, and we can also bring in metric inventory for volume orders.

## Metric size availability

| Product | Metric size |
| --- | --- |
| SP10 | M10 |
| SP12 | M10 |
| SP18 | M12 |
| SP58 | M16 |

All metric products are available in Zinc or Stainless variants.
